Jenna Fields

Scholar LinkedIn

Jenna Fields is a PhD student in Computational Precision Health. She completed her undergraduate education at Cornell, majoring in Computer Science, English, and an independent major through the College Scholar Program. Before beginning her PhD, she worked as a software engineer at Amazon’s Project Kuiper. Her research interests include treatment optimization in oncology, with a particular focus on glioblastoma, and value of information in clinical decision-making.
