MD/MS Candidate

Niloufar Golchini

UC Berkeley–UCSF Joint Medical Program

Scholar LinkedIn Twitter/X

Niloufar Golchini is a medical student at the UCSF School of Medicine and UC Berkeley Joint Medical Program. She did her undergraduate degree at UC Berkeley focusing on Metabolic Biology and Data Science. Recently, her work has focused on real-world benchmarking of LLMs in medicine, developing machine learning methods for clinical reasoning, and the evaluation and translation of AI tools in the clinical workflow.
