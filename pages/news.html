We are proud to announce that Ida Sim, MD, PhD, and researchers' proposal for the Center for Edge Medicine has been selected as one of eight winners of the Laude Institute's Moonshots grant… We are proud to announce that Ida Sim, MD, PhD, and researchers' proposal for the Center for Edge Medicine has been selected as one of eight winners of the Laude Institute's Moonshots grant — a competitive initiative funding AI research to tackle some of humanity's hardest problems. The $250,000 seed grant supports six months of focused work building open-source AI infrastructure for health for frontline healthcare: the kind that works in patients' daily lives, not just clinics and hospitals. The project was selected under the program's Frontline Healthcare domain, which focuses on AI applications to support diagnosis, treatment, and patient care.
